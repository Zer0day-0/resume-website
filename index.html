Hello World, I am Saurav, a self taught NGS-Programmer based out in Awizal, Mizoram, India. Currently persuing PhD in Mizoram University. Previously, I graduated from KIIT University, Bhubaneswar in 2021. When Not exhaused from hiking in the mountains, or fiddling with computers, I read research papers. I like eating good food, riding bikes and exploring cool stuff. Shell, Python, Rust and R are my tools of choice. I also happen to write some HTML5, CSS and JS, but I'm nowhere near a pro.
I work in RNA sequencing (RNA-seq), which is a powerful next-generation sequencing (NGS) technique used to analyze the transcriptome, capturing the complete set of RNA transcripts in a sample. Studying the transcriptome gives us a deeper insight into active genes and provides details about gene expression patterns, alternative splicing events, and regulatory mechanisms involved in diseases.
I am highly experienced in DNA sequencing analysis and variant calling, which are essential components of next-generation sequencing (NGS). My expertise includes preprocessing raw sequencing data, alignment, quality control, and applying advanced tools like GATK and DRAGEN pipelines. Through this experience, I have contributed to projects such as the analysis of Genome India data.
